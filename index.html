"The Force Awakens": Continuing the Legacy

JJ Abrams and Disney recently took on the challenge of contining George Lucas's legacy by exceedings the expectations of Star Wars fans with the seventh installment of the franchise.

The Cast

Influenced by modern times and the everchanging focus of society, "The Force Awakens" features a strong female lead, companionship in the form of an African American man, and Adam Driver of GIRLS fame. These new additions are supplemented by tributes to the original triloqy, made in the form of Han Solo and Leia's revisited romance, spoken legends of Luke Skywalker, and, of course, the traditional scrolling introduction that sets the stage for the movie's premise.

Other revived members of the orginal cast include:

  • R2D2
  • C3PO
  • Chewbacca
